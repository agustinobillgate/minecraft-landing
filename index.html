THE ADVENTURE

In the world of Minecraft, legends speak of the Order of the Stone—five heroes who defeated the legendary Ender Dragon and saved the Overworld. But those heroes are long gone, and new threats emerge from the shadows.

When a dangerous experiment goes wrong at EnderCon, a monstrous Wither Storm is unleashed upon the world. Jesse and their friends must embark on a perilous journey to find the Order of the Stone and discover the secret to destroying the beast before it consumes everything in its path.
